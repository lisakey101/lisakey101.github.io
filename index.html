location

Eastnor Castle Deer Park, Herefordshire

El Dorado Festival will be taking place in the beautiful grounds of the Eastnor Castle Deer Park in Ledbury, Herefordshire.

Covered in fields, lakes and woodland it truly is one of the most beautiful sites we've ever seen.

camping
